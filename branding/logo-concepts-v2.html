Concept 01
The Folding Paradox
An Escher-inspired impossible document that folds through itself. The page creates a Penrose-triangle-like construction where the fold passes through its own plane. The cyan-to-amber gradient marks the paradox intersection point.
Impossible geometry creates a cognitive hook — your brain tries to resolve the shape and can't. Communicates "we go THROUGH your documents, not just over them."

Concept 02
The Dissolving Page
A document disintegrates into a cloud of particles flowing in a Fibonacci spiral pattern. Raw document goes in, pure data intelligence comes out. A hidden "A" shape (Agent) is visible in the brighter particle cluster.
Documents don't just get "read" — they get decomposed into intelligence. The cyan-to-violet particle gradient represents transformation from raw content to AI insight.

Concept 03
The Negative Space Agent
A bold "dA" lettermark where the gap between the letters forms an eye/lens shape — the "agent" watching through your documents. The amber pupil blinks occasionally, bringing the mark to life.
Negative space logos are the most remembered logos in history (FedEx arrow, NBC peacock). The "discovery moment" when you see the eye is unforgettable. Communicates "intelligent agent hidden within documents."

Concept 04
The Mesh Bloom
A living, breathing gradient mesh inside a rounded square — like Stripe's aesthetic but with a ghost document shape emerging from the color flow. Four color control points continuously orbit, creating an ever-shifting brand mark.
Generative logos are the cutting-edge 2026 trend. Each instance is slightly different — the brand is recognized by its color DNA rather than fixed geometry. The mesh hides a document ghost that appears and disappears.

Concept 05
The Typographic Illusion
No icon. No symbol. Just "docAgent" constructed from isometric 3D letterforms — each letter is an architectural beam floating in space. A cyan laser line runs through all letters at the same depth. Reads as text from afar, reveals impossible 3D construction up close.
Typography-as-logo is the premium tier of branding (Cursor Gothic, OpenAI Sans). The depth illusion communicates "we see deeper into your documents." The word itself IS the logo — no icon needed.

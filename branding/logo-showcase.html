Design Concept
Logo Anatomy
Document shape — Rounded rectangle with corner fold. Universally recognized as "document". Contains text lines representing content being analyzed.
AI Spark — 8-pointed starburst in the bottom-right corner, partially overlapping the document edge. Represents AI intelligence "illuminating" the document. The glow effect suggests active processing.
Wordmark: doc — Lowercase, clean, approachable. "doc" = document. Set in Space Grotesk Bold for geometric precision.
Wordmark: AI — Uppercase, gradient-filled, distinctive. The gradient (Electric Blue → Violet) signals intelligence and modernity. The case shift (doc → AI) creates visual rhythm.
